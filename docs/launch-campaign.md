## 3. Suggested sequencing

1. Founder LinkedIn post (day 1) — drives initial signups and credibility.
2. Email to any existing waitlist/contacts (day 1–2).
3. X thread breaking down one real feature per day (days 2–6) — WARDOG,
   R-E-A-D, Wallet, Comms Hub, Academy.
4. Short-form video or screen recording of the product (week 2) once a few
   real beta users have given feedback you can quote.

Keep every post honest about beta status — "beta," "founding member," and
"early access" framing is doing real work here: it explains why there's no
huge user base yet, and turns that into an incentive instead of a weakness.
