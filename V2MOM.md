# FASS Flow — V2MOM

*One page. Read it before adding anything.*

---

## Vision
*Where we're going, in one sentence.*

**Every capable small business can win government work — without a grant writer, a consultant, or a decade of learning the paperwork.** FASS Flow is the operating system that takes a service business from "I didn't know I could bid" to "we won, and we delivered."

---

## Values
*What we will not trade away, in priority order.*

1. **Trust.** Customers bet their business on us. Their data is safe, the system is up, nothing they enter ever disappears. (Non-negotiable — it's the whole product.)
2. **Customer outcome over feature count.** We measure ourselves in contracts *they* win, not tabs *we* ship.
3. **Plain English.** The govcon world hides behind jargon. We translate. Always.
4. **Access.** We exist to open a $700B+ market to the businesses historically locked out of it.

---

## Methods
*The few things we actually do this quarter.*

1. **Win the one workflow: Find → Qualify → Respond → Win.** WARDOG → R-E-A-D → FILL → Pipeline is *the* product. Make that single path undeniably the best on earth. Everything else (Estimator, Foreman, Restoration, WITNESS) is expansion — it waits.
2. **One record, full history.** Every tool reads and writes the same opportunity object, with a complete audit trail. (Started: Pipeline change-tracking.)
3. **Make the win visible.** Total dollars won, per customer, on the dashboard. The Awarded view is the trophy case.
4. **Education as the front door.** The Masterclass turns strangers into qualified users and users into evangelists.

---

## Obstacles
*What will stop us if we don't handle it.*

- **Sprawl.** ~12 tools, not enough depth in the one that earns money. *Antidote: focus, ruthlessly.*
- **Infrastructure fragility.** (Largely fixed — the app now lives in a real, backed-up repo, not a disposable folder.)
- **Compliance ceiling.** CMMC / data handling will gate bigger customers. Plan for it before it blocks a deal.
- **Founder bandwidth.** Building alone. Every "let's also add…" has a real cost.

---

## Measures
*The numbers that tell us it's working. One matters most.*

> ### ⭐ North Star: **$ of government contracts won by FASS Flow customers.**

Supporting:
- Opportunities flagged → bids submitted → **bids won** (the funnel, end to end)
- Active customers who won at least one contract (the proof)
- Time from "found it" to "submitted" (the leverage we add)

---

*If a new idea doesn't serve the North Star or the one workflow, it goes on a someday list — not in the product this quarter.*
