# FASS Flow — The Operating System for Government Contracting

> Find the work. Win the bid. Get it done.

FASS Flow helps small businesses find winnable government contracts, decide whether to bid, cut proposal work from weeks to hours, execute the awarded work, and get paid — all in one platform. Built by FASS Technologies LLC, based in Baltimore, MD. Now in public beta.

## One platform, three lanes

### Win Work
Find the right opportunities, decide which ones are worth pursuing, and build a compliant bid.

- **FASS Academy** — Structured courses on SAM.gov registration, NAICS codes, set-aside programs, and the full procurement lifecycle.
- **WARDOG** — AI-powered opportunity scanner that matches open solicitations to your capabilities and past performance, delivered daily.
- **R-E-A-D** — A six-question scoring workflow (eligibility, requirements, capacity, timing, margin, substantiation) before you spend an hour writing.
- **FASS FILL** — Guided proposal builder with smart templates, compliance checklists, and pricing tools so your response meets every FAR requirement.

### Execute Work
Perform the awarded contract and build the documented track record that wins the next one.

- **Auto-fill** — Auto-fill SAM.gov registrations, certifications, and contract forms from your stored profile. One click, done.
- **Execution paperwork** — Schedule of values, AIA pay apps, RFIs, submittals, and daily logs — the paperwork a federal job actually requires.
- **Contractor Camera** — Room-by-room or line-item estimating with photo verification, so the same list doubles as a work-progress tracker.
- **WITNESS** — Track deliverables, manage invoicing, and document performance so every award builds the past-performance record for the next pursuit.

### Grow Customers
Government work isn't your only revenue. FASS Wallet runs loyalty and repeat business for everyone else you serve.

- **Digital stamp/gift cards** — Stamp cards and gift cards customers actually keep on their phone — no app to download, just tap to add.
- **Customer messaging** — Send a push notification straight to every customer's Wallet pass, backed by a real customer list — bonus stamps, offers, reminders.
