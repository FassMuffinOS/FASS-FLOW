# FASS Flow Releases — Shipped, Grouped, Real

The same changelog as /updates, organized by release area instead of by date — what shipped, and why it matters to the business using it.

## Win Work — AI-Scored Opportunities
Shipped: AI win-likelihood score and Win-Delta badge on every WARDOG opportunity; full solicitation text and PDF attachments captured automatically on save; searchable NAICS code picker with a larger, paginated result set.
Impact: Less time spent reading the wrong contracts.

## Grow Customers — FASS Wallet: Gift Cards & Payouts
Shipped: Digital gift cards delivered as real Apple Wallet passes; public no-login storefront; Stripe Connect payouts straight to the business's own account.
Impact: Businesses can sell gift cards online without building their own storefront or payments integration.

## Grow Customers — Wallet Campaigns & Rewards
Shipped: Push loyalty offers and updated rewards directly to a customer's Apple Wallet pass; digital stamp cards with tap-to-join and staff redemption; customer list/CRM panel with targeted sends.
Impact: No separate loyalty app — the offer updates on a pass customers already carry.

## Execute Jobs — Messenger v2
Shipped: File attachments, emoji reactions, message editing, read receipts; real browser push notifications and presence; opportunities/proposals/profile cards shareable as live cards in chat.
Impact: One inbox for teaming conversations instead of texts, email, and DMs spread across apps.

## Execute Jobs — Teaming & Business Profiles
Shipped: Group/teaming threads with three or more participants; capability-based people search by NAICS code, certification, or past performance; public discoverable Business Profile pages.
Impact: Finding a subcontractor or teaming partner is a search, not cold outreach.

## Grow Customers — Comms Hub & Team Up
Shipped: A single threaded SMS inbox with nudge alerts; Team Up partner-network board with built-in chat.
Impact: Customer and partner conversations live in FASS Flow instead of a personal phone.

## Grow Customers — Affiliate Program
Shipped: Referral signup with commission tracking on every real payment; gamified dashboard with levels, XP, and sub-affiliate recruiting; shareable social cards.
Impact: Businesses and partners earn by referring others, with payout math handled automatically.

## Win Work — Smarter Dashboard
Shipped: Business Health score from real account activity; AI-written daily brief; AI Chief-of-Staff query box.
Impact: Opening the dashboard answers "what should I do today," not just raw numbers.

## Company — Careers & Growth Challenge
Shipped: Public Careers page with an in-app application flow; Growth Challenge gamified milestones with automatic rewards.
Impact: Hiring and account growth both get milestone-driven, in-product treatment.

## Company — New Public Site
Shipped: Rebuilt landing page, About, Press, Blog; beta-launch messaging and founding-member offer; SEO/AEO foundation (sitemap, robots.txt, llms.txt, structured data).
Impact: The site explains what FASS Flow actually does instead of leaning on placeholder marketing.
