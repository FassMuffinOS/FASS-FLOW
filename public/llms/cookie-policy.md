# Cookie Policy

*Last updated: June 28, 2026*

This policy describes the cookies and similar storage technologies FASS Flow uses, and your choices around them.

## Essential — Authentication

A Supabase Auth session token is used to keep you signed in. There is no opt-out, since the service cannot function without it.

## Functional — Affiliate attribution

If you arrive via a `?ref=` link, the referral code is stored in your browser's local storage for up to 30 days to attribute signups to the correct affiliate. This is not shared with ad networks.

## What we don't use

FASS Flow does not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or retargeting.

## Managing cookies and local storage

You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings at any time; doing so will sign you out and clear stored preferences.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
