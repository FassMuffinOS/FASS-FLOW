- **Sequential unlock** — Mission 2 doesn't open until Mission 1 is marked complete.
- **Real homework, every mission** — Each mission ends with an assignment tied to your actual business — your NAICS code, your solicitation, your price.
- **Printable worksheets** — Every mission has a downloadable worksheet with the exact fields to fill in.
- **Certificate of Completion** — Finish all 10 missions and get a Certificate of Completion from FASS Technologies LLC.
