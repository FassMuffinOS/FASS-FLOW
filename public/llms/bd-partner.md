# FASS BD Partner Program

> More than opportunity alerts. A partner from readiness to performance.

Daily WARDOG alerts. Same-day bid/no-bid discipline. One active proposal at a time, assembled correctly. A monthly operating relationship, not a subscription to a list.

**$500/month** · month-to-month · 30-day written notice to cancel. No percentage-of-award fee. No long-term contract. The client authorizes every submission.

## What's included

- **WARDOG Opportunity Scan** — Daily WARDOG alerts plus 2 bid/no-bid reviews per month. FASS monitors SAM.gov, Maryland eMMA, and other public procurement sources every day for notices that match your approved capability profile.
- **Bid/no-bid scoring** — Every flagged opportunity goes through the six-question R-E-A-D workflow: eligibility, mandatory requirements, capacity, timing, margin, and substantiation.
- **Response planning** — FASS maps solicitation instructions, evaluation factors, staffing needs, technical approach, compliance items, and pricing inputs into a controlled response plan before any writing starts.
- **Proposal assembly support** — Technical narrative development, staffing plan organization, pricing support, cover-letter preparation, and required-form tracking — one active solicitation response at a time, included in the monthly plan.
- **FASS Flow platform access** — Full access to your pipeline tracker, opportunity worksheet, registration checklist, capability profile workspace, and response dashboard while you are engaged.
- **Monthly pipeline review** — A structured review of your active opportunities, bid decisions made, and pursuit status.

## Your first 30 days

1. **Week 1 — Capability profile + NAICS positioning.** We lock in your real NAICS codes, geography, set-aside status, and delivery capacity so WARDOG only flags work you can actually perform.
2. **Week 2 — Opportunity filters + WARDOG setup.** Your daily sweep goes live across SAM.gov, Maryland eMMA, and other public sources, tuned to your capability profile.
3. **Week 3 — First bid/no-bid review.** We run your first flagged opportunity through the six-question R-E-A-D scoring workflow together.
4. **Week 4 — Active proposal plan + monthly strategy review.** One opportunity moves into an active response plan, and we set the cadence for your recurring monthly pipeline review.

## The operating system

Every opportunity evaluated builds your bid discipline. Every completed solicitation becomes structured past-performance evidence for the next pursuit. FASS does not sell a list of opportunities and disappear — we build a repeatable bidding and execution system with you. The client remains the prime contractor and authorizes every submission.

Modules: WARDOG (opportunity intelligence), R-E-A-D (bid discipline), FASS FILL (execution capacity), WITNESS (closeout proof).
