# About FASS Flow

> The Operating System for Small Business Government Contracting

FASS Flow is a small team building the platform that helps small businesses find government work, decide whether to bid, win it, execute it, and get paid — end to end, in one place. We're in public beta right now, which means the people signing up today are shaping what we build next, and locking in founding pricing while they're at it.

## Why we exist

Most small-business tooling is built for enterprises with procurement teams, not a five-person crew trying to grow. We started FASS Flow because finding, winning, and running government work is full of friction that has nothing to do with doing good work — and almost all of it is fixable with software.

## Our mission

- Help small businesses find real, winnable government contracts
- Tell them honestly whether a bid is worth their time before they spend it
- Cut proposal and compliance work from weeks to hours
- Give them the tools to execute, document, and close out the work
- Help them get paid faster and grow their customer base while they do it

## Our values

- **Ship Fast** — Working software beats a perfect plan. We ship, we learn, we ship again.
- **Solve Real Problems** — Every feature traces back to an actual small business stuck on an actual problem.
- **Customer First** — We talk to the people using this every week, not just at launch.
- **Learn Constantly** — Government contracting, AI, design — nobody here knew all of this on day one.
- **Ownership** — You'll own a real piece of the product, not a ticket queue.
